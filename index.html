The Dark Night event!

Posted by Ghott0 on Mar 25

The Dark Night event in the Assetto Corsa Competizione sim series is coming up on april 16. The event consists of a 10 Hour night race around the Suzuka circuit. There are currently 50 teams signed up. Ghott0 is the owner of 2 of them, racing under the Crashalots crew.
